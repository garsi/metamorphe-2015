Yoga Peak Adventures

One woman's fantastic vision for uniting the outdoors and yoga needed a brand and a website.

Where do we sign up?!

When we first heard Erin, the owner of Yoga Peak Adventures, talk about her idea to merge group hikes with yoga and meditation we just had one question: where do we sign up?

And that's how this project started.

Erin needed a few things, and she has a great vision for where she wants to take her idea. She had the logo design, the colors, the web requirements:

  • be able to sign up for events
  • buy merchandise
  • write a blog about her adventures
  • sign up for her mailing list
  • look awesome

She just didn't have the time to work on that side of things while working full-time, teaching yoga, being an amazing mother and wife, and setting up all the other pieces of her new business.

We were thrilled to be able to work on this project for Erin and it's still in the works. Here's a preview of some of the things we've been working on for her brand and website.

Logo

Who's that lady dancer posing in the center? It's Erin! We believe in using a lot of authentic qualities in our client's brands and there's a story behind many elements.

Colors

Erin's vision includes a cool color scheme blending purples, navies and teals. One of Erin's (and our) favorite hikes is Lake Serene. Her hiking photos of the area inspired the palette.

Fonts

Erin prefers a very modern and open look. We found a few fonts that have that combination of natural quality and clean lines for her logo and website.

Prototype

We're building out a prototype using Bootstrap with the intention of programming it into a WordPress theme. We still have some code to write and you can take a peek here.
